# The node_modules Iceberg: Mysteries Below the Waterline

What lurks below the tip of the iceberg? Well, if you look at it from the aspect of web and software, it is our best friend, the node_modules. Have you ever jumped into your node_modules and looked around to see what the various packages of your packages are? Chances are, no. We don't have time for that...but we should! Let's dive into the world of transitive dependencies and break down what they are. In this session, you will learn what transitive dependencies are, how to identify them, the risks they pose, and how to integrate TuxCare's ELS solution to address them.
